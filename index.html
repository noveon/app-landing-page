Features & Capabilities

  • Automatic 30-day reminders for item usage tracking.
  • Inventory management with color-coded statuses:
    • Green: Regularly used items.
    • Yellow: Items needing attention.
    • Red: Items recommended for removal.
  • Push notifications at 30, 60, 90, and 100 days.
  • Easy decision-making: confirm usage or remove unused items.
  • Filter and sort items by category and usage status.
  • Database-backed tracking with dynamically updated reminder intervals.
