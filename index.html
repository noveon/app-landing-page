Features & Capabilities

  • Automatic 30-day reminders for item usage tracking.
  • Inventory management with color-coded statuses:
    • Green: Regularly used items
    • Yellow: Items needing attention
    • Red: Items recommended for removal
  • Push notifications at 30, 60, 90, and 100 days.
  • Easy decision-making: confirm usage or remove unused items.
  • Filter and sort items by category and usage status.
  • Database-backed tracking with dynamically updated reminder intervals.

How It Works

Our app is designed to help you focus on what truly matters by tracking your items’ usage and prompting you at optimal intervals. Here's an overview:

  • Initial Setup: Each item is added with a default 30-day reminder.
  • Usage Reminders: After 30 days, you receive a prompt asking, “Have you used [Item] in the last 30 days?”
  • Adaptive Intervals: Confirming usage increases the interval (e.g., from 30 to 60 days) and updates in the database.
  • Removal Prompts: After 90 days of non-use, a prompt asks if you’d like to remove the item. Keeping it resets the reminder; discarding it removes the item.
  • Automatic Flagging: At 100 days of non-use, the item is flagged for removal with a final prompt.
  • Inventory Overview: Items are color-coded (green, yellow, red) based on usage, with filtering options to help you focus on what matters.

This system ensures you maintain a minimalist lifestyle by retaining only the items that are truly essential.
